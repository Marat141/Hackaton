# 4. Talking About Where You're From

Základní fráze:

- **I’m from (city/country).**
- **I’m (nationality).**
- **We’re from (city/country).**

Příklady:

- _I’m from Manchester in Great Britain. I’m British._
- _We’re from Constanta in Romania. We’re Romanian._
- _I’m from Casablanca in Morocco. I’m Moroccan._

# 5. Grammar: Verb _BE_ (am / is / are)

## Affirmative form

- I am → **I’m**
- You are → **you’re**
- He/She/It is → **he’s / she’s / it’s**
- We/You/They are → **we’re / you’re / they’re**

## Negative form

- I am not → **I’m not**
- You/We/They are not → **aren’t**
- He/She/It is not → **isn’t**

## Interrogative form (otázky)

- Am I …?
- Are you …?
- Is he/she/it …?
- Are we/they …?

## Short answers

- Yes, I am. / No, I’m not.
- Yes, he is. / No, he isn’t.
- Yes, we are. / No, we aren’t.

# 6. Subject Pronouns (podmětná zájmena)

| Singular      | Plural |
| ------------- | ------ |
| I             | we     |
| you           | you    |
| he / she / it | they   |

Použití:

- _He_ is for males.
- _She_ is for females.
- _It_ is for animals or things.

---

# 7. Possessive Adjectives (přivlastňovací přídavná jména)

| Singular        | Plural |
| --------------- | ------ |
| my              | our    |
| your            | your   |
| his / her / its | their  |

Příklad:

- _Molly and **her** backpack_
- _Zac and **his** mother_
- _This is **my** school._

# 13. Pronunciation – Intonation in Questions

### Yes/No questions → stoupavá intonace (↗)

_Are you from France? ↗_

### WH- questions → klesavá intonace (↘)

_Where are you from? ↘_
